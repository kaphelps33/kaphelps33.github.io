About

At Kempt Platypus, we pride ourselves on crafting high-quality, leather bound books that bring people the charm and timeless elegance of a bygone era. Each of our fine leather bound books is meticulously hand-bound using the finest quality leather that is designed to age gracefully over time, much like the treasured volumes you might find at a local antique shop. Our collection spans from classic literature to more modern day classics, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern day needs. Weather you are a collector, or simply an avid enjoyer, we pride ourselves with being able to meet your needs and high standards.
