Project Overview

The application will serve as a platform for individuals or groups to plan travel itineraries, discover destinations, and gather information about various travel packages. Users can interact with interactive elements like destination galleries, weather info, and customizable itineraries.

The website's intended users are people who wish to have their travels planned for them, people who seek travel recommendations or specialized information, or tourists who need travel advice or services. The website's content will be a home page, a landing page for current or future clients, a destinations page where users can view different travel areas and a contact page where a user can begin looking for planning services.

Site Map

Page Design

  • Purpose: Overview of the site, featured trips, and news.
  • Audience: General users.
  • Content:
    • Sliders with images of destinations
    • Featured trips and promotions
    • Brief descriptions of popular destinations
  • Interactivity:
    • Slideshow of popular destinations
    • Buttons linking to other pages

Destinations Page

  • Purpose: Showcase available travel destinations with detailed information.
  • Audience: Travelers seeking information.
  • Content:
    • Clickable destination cards
    • Image galleries with lightbox effect
    • Descriptions and highlights of destinations
  • Interactivity:
    • Click on destination cards to view details
    • Enlarge images with lightbox effect

Itinerary Page

  • Purpose: Allow users to plan their trips.
  • Audience: Registered users/customers.
  • Content:
    • Form to select destinations
    • Form to select dates and activities
  • Interactivity:
    • Date picker (jQuery UI)
    • Destination selection dropdown

Contact Page

  • Purpose: Provide contact details for inquiries and personalized planning.
  • Audience: All users.
  • Content:
    • Contact form for inquiries
    • Email address and phone number
  • Interactivity: Submit button with form validation.

About Page

  • Purpose: Provide information about services for general users.
  • Audience: All users.
  • Content:
    • Why the site was created and general biographical information
  • Interactivity: Simple navigation and read-only content.
